## THE ONE IDEA EVERYTHING HANGS ON

The government has banned social media for under 16s. The access date keeps moving. That is the delay. To most parents it sounds like protection. It is not. It is a cliff edge.

A child banned at 13 turns 16 and walks off the edge into full access overnight, with no skills, no framework, and no supervised transition. The ban handles access. It does not handle preparation. Preparation is a pathway, built from age 4 to 16, one stage at a time, so that 16 is a step and not a fall.

That is the cornerstone idea. Everything below is the same idea cut for a different room.

**The line that travels:** *The ban is a wall. A wall is not a plan. We build the bridge from 4 to 16.*

---

## WHY A WATERFALL AND NOT SCATTERED POSTS

You write the deep piece once. The newsletter is the reservoir. Every other format is water drawn from it.

- The **newsletter** holds the full argument and the full pathway. It is the thing you link to.
- The **LinkedIn series** breaks the argument into one idea per post for the professional and schools audience.
- The **Facebook series** answers the real questions parents are already asking, in a parent voice, and quietly points home.
- The **Instagram series** turns each idea into a visual you can hold, in the hand drawn evidence style.
- The **infographics** are the cornerstone visuals that get saved and reshared on every platform.
- The **playbook** is the thing parents and schools keep. It is the value you give before you ask for anything.

Write the reservoir first. Then open the taps in order.

Run this over three weeks, same rhythm as the ban series distribution plan. The opportunity window is open while the ban is in the news. Move while it is loud.

**Week 1 · set the reservoir, open the wall idea**
- Day 1: publish the newsletter on Substack and as a LinkedIn newsletter. Email the subscriber list with a personal note and the link.
- Day 1: LinkedIn post 1 (the wall is not a plan). Link to the newsletter in the first comment, never the body.

- LinkedIn posts 7 to 8 (the parent action post, the closer).

- Assess what converts. Boost the best performing organic post. Double down on the format that is working.

## VOICE RULES FOR EVERY WORD IN THIS WATERFALL

1. Justin's voice. Warm, plain, direct. No jargon, no AI tells.
2. No dashes anywhere. Spell ranges with the word to. Age 4 to 16, under 16s, not a wall.
3. Lead with usefulness, not critique. Give the value before the point.
4. Never allow or deny. The pathway is always a calibrated next step.
5. The crisis is real. Never dismiss the concern. Disagree with the mechanism, not the worry.
6. Cite the source when you make a claim. Parents trust evidence they can check.
