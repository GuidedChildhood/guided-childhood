# How to use this pack — Not the phones, verified (In Proportion series)

Built from the verified V2 briefing (six lenses, every citation checked, 17 July 2026). Every claim traces to a confirmed ledger entry. Two demoted claims, Gray's video game suicide attribution and the misattributed Trevor Project figures, are banned from all copy here.

## The one line
It is not the phones, and it is not only the phones. Neither a blanket ban nor a free for all fits the evidence. The calibrated pathway that targets the pattern of use and the whole family, rather than the device, is the only position the data supports.

## Sequencing note
The Substack issue is the source of truth. The LinkedIn posts each carry one brick of it. Do not fire all five in one week, spread them across two so the series builds. Two pieces state the underlying thesis plainly, the newsletter and Post 5, so they are staggered a week apart. Only one plain landing goes out per week. Keep that rhythm to roughly one in ten across the wider calendar so the thesis stays a slow reveal, not a headline.

## Mission filter watch item

On its own it reads as screens do not matter, which is the opposite of the point. The guardrail does not travel with the quote.
