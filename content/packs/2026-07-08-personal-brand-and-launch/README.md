# Personal brand and app launch plan

Two Google Docs in the Guided Childhood Research folder are the canonical versions:
- "GDC — Personal brand and app launch plan (Starter Story research)"
- "GDC — The starter story (founder narrative and build-in-public launch spine)"

Built from Starter Story research (Pat Walls). This README is the repo record and quick reference.

## The core move
Audience first. Justin's personal brand is the pre launch audience for Guided Digital Childhood. Build in public into the playlist and GDC parent launch. The owned email list, not any social platform, is the real asset. Cleanest verified exemplar: Tweet Hunter (Tibo Louis-Lucas), audience first to product.

## Brand and voice (lock before B P or AI produces assets)
Brand line: the voice of children's mental health and of teaching digital and AI devices through a calibrated, age based pathway. Not fear, not a ban, capability.
Pillars: the calm read; the pathway; the parent who did the homework.
Voice rules: UK English, short sentences, no dashes, no AI isms, concede the strongest counter point, one precise number as the hook, never scare always equip, credit before you complicate, never relitigate the ban.
Visual: Guided Childhood tokens (cream, green, coral, gold, lav), Hanken Grotesk plus IBM Plex Mono, chunky rounded, no generic AI gradients.

## Pipeline (one idea, five rooms plus video)
Idea to LinkedIn post to carousel to reel/short (Higgsfield) to Substack to Reddit/Mumsnet/Facebook native. B P owns FB/IG, carousels, reels, schools and parent group outreach from the shared Drive. Reddit and Mumsnet stay Justin's own voice.

## Platform split
LinkedIn (researchers, teachers, heads), FB/IG/reels (parents), Reddit and Mumsnet (Justin's voice), schools and parent groups (B P outreach, compliance and evidence angle). Newsletter sits under all of it.

## Launch roadmap (build in public)
Wk1-2 lock brand, turn on waitlist and lead magnet, start process posts. Wk3-4 cornerstone content seeded value first, playlists live as a process moment. Wk5-6 founder rate (50 cap, real scarcity) to the waitlist first, GDC parent launch to the owned list. Measure the funnel (email signups, waitlist to founder conversions), not followers.

## Higgsfield
Connector is live in the workspace; reels/UGC from posts is testable. Run a handful of real tests before committing a pipeline.

## Honest caveats
Survivorship bias (Starter Story shows winners); attention is not willingness to pay; consistency is years not weeks; the content brand and product must serve the same need (yours do). Validate demand with waitlist and lead magnet numbers early.
