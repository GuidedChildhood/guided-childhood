# The better game (rewards system post)

Canonical: Google Doc "GDC — The better game (rewards system post, contrarian, long)".

Full length contrarian LinkedIn post explaining the app's rewards system (play outside, chores, earned digital time) through real behavioural science. Contrarian cold open (we pay kids to go outside and scientists wince), deep science (displacement and Gray free play, Premack principle, variable rewards turned on the real world), honest pivot (overjustification effect can kill intrinsic motivation), resolution (Self Determination Theory: rewards designed to fade into autonomy, competence, relatedness). Bribe versus bridge as the contrast. Hidden Thread brick: the children with least to fall back on at home. Link in the first comment, not the body, for LinkedIn reach.

Science flag: Gray (Free to Learn 2013) and Ofcom 2024 are Justin's cited sources; Premack 1959, overjustification (Deci 1971; Lepper et al 1973) and Self Determination Theory (Deci and Ryan) are established textbook psychology, not freshly fact checked this session.
