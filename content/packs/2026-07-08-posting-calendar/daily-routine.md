Start Friday: Algorithm Post 1 (strongest lineage) + first comment. Sat Mumsnet Accurate Story. Sun founder starter story. Mon Algorithm Post 3 + reel. Tue reply. Wed Accurate Story Post 1 + carousel. Thu Accurate Story Substack + Facebook. Then Master calendar Week 2.
