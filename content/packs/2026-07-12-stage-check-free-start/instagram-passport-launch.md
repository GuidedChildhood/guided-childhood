## The mechanic, in one breath

A follower taps the invite link in the bio or the post. They land on a page that feels made for them (You have been invited). Their child gets a Digital Passport with the first quests already unlocked, free, as a taste. To keep going and unlock the full passport they start the 14 day free trial, no card needed, framed as being one of the early families who give feedback. It is warm, it is exclusive to this audience, and it turns 6,000 old balloon customers into the platform's first real families.

Why a passport: it is a concrete, lovely object a child earns stamp by stamp as they walk the pathway from 4 to 16. It makes an abstract subscription feel like a real thing your child holds and fills in. It is the something we wish existed line made physical.

## Notes and honest flags

- **The pivot is a strength, not a risk, told this way.** Balloons for kids, now the harder kids problem. It is the same family looking after the same families. That story earns trust.
- **Test then commit.** Post these three before you rename the account. If the mums respond, rename to Guided Childhood and keep all 6,000 followers. If they are quiet, you learned it cheaply.
- **One offer per launch.** The Digital Passport plus 14 day trial is the whole ask. Do not also push the founder rate here, it muddies it. Founder rate is for the public launch.
- **Feedback is the real prize.** These 6,000 are worth more as your first honest testers than as quick signups. The trial framed as help us make it right is true and it converts.
