## The one hook and the one link

Every piece carries the same hook: **what stage is your child**. Every route ends at the same place: **guidedchildhood.co.uk/starter-pack**. Do not change either while you are still learning what works. One hook, one link, everywhere.

Space the forum posts out. Posting the same idea in five places in one hour looks coordinated, which is what gets you removed.

## Who each piece is for, and what to expect

- **LinkedIn (parents plus schools):** your warmest audience and your fastest traffic. Expect the evidence post (3) and the script (4) to travel furthest. The comment keyword STAGE is both your reach engine and your CTA, so reply to every one fast.
- **Substack (your list, the committed ones):** this is where the whole argument lives in one place. People who read it are your future founders. The link is in the body here because it is your own list, not a hostile feed.
- **Mumsnet (worried parents, high trust, zero tolerance for marketing):** no link unless asked. If it reads as an advert it gets shredded, and that is worse than silence. You are a parent thinking out loud.
- **Reddit (evidence minded parents and teachers):** sources stay in, promotion stays out. On r/ScienceBasedParenting every claim needs its link. Lead with the question, disclose the product only if asked.
- **Facebook groups (local and national parents):** warm, short, one idea, a question at the end. Link is usually fine, but the value comes first.

## The one rule for forums (do not skip)

Be a parent who helps, roughly one in ten posts mentions the tool. That is the mission filter and the one in ten rule from the content engine. In practice: answer other people's phone and screen questions with real, warm, linkless replies for a few days before you ever post your own. You are earning the right to be there. Break it and the group bans you, and a banned account reaches nobody.

## What to measure

Every day, in your one sheet, log signups and quiz starts next to what you posted. By Friday you will see which pillar pulls, the cliff, the ban, the evidence, the script, or the founder story. That answer is worth more than the signups themselves, because it tells you what to hand a creator when you are ready to spend.
