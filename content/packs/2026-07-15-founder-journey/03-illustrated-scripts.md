# The illustrated scripts — read aloud, drawings over the top

This is the format you described. You read the script as voiceover (or straight to camera), and we lay the hand drawn lesson style illustrations over it, the DiGi Squad look, warm line drawings, the pathway, the stage icons. No AI text on screen. Captions burned from your real voiceover.

**How to read these:** the plain text is what you say. Each `[DRAW: ...]` is the illustration or simple animation that appears while you say the line before it. Keep your delivery slow and warm. Each script is 60 to 110 seconds.

**Illustration style guide (same as the lessons):** simple warm hand drawn lines, the cream and green palette, DiGi as the friendly guide, a child figure, a parent figure, the five stage pathway as a gentle climbing path, no stock photos, no charts unless they are hand drawn. Movement is subtle, a line drawing itself in, a soft fade, never flashy.
