# Splash! Match the Fish to Their Colour — product folder

Recipe: early learning colour matching game (printables-engine, near the
tracing and fine motor lane, DiGi fronting for a whole family early years
product). Price rung £2.99. Evergreen, ages 2 to 5.

Built 21 July 2026 by the printables-engine pipeline. Fronted by DiGi, the
golden star, in DiGi Junior "BEEP BOOP" voice for the little ones. Concept
replicates the La Casita Educativa "Colorful fish tank" colour matching
activity, rebuilt whole in the Guided Childhood house style. No third party
art, no brand named.

## The format: colour plus colour in plus tips
The headline feature, same engine as the back to school escape. One HTML
source (print.html, generated by gen-fish.mjs) renders three PDFs:
- `product/colour-splash-fish-tank-colour-A4.pdf` — full colour, A4, 8 pages.
- `product/colour-splash-fish-tank-colour-USLetter.pdf` — full colour, US Letter, 8 pages.
- `product/colour-splash-fish-tank-colour-in-A4.pdf` — line art edition, 9 pages
  (the 8 pages as bold black colourable outlines, plus DiGi's colouring tips
  page). Made by adding body class `colourin`, which strips every decorative
  SVG fill to white with a strong ink outline (non scaling stroke so the line
  weight is even at any size), turns the water tints and colour chips white,
  and swaps the DiGi photo for a line art star. Text stays solid ink so it is
  always readable. Eye pupils carry class `keep` so they stay solid, and small
  glints carry `noline` so they vanish cleanly.

In the colour in edition the whole product becomes a colour and match game:
the child colours a fish and its bowl the same colour, then matches them. The
colour name labels stay as ink words so the child knows which colour to reach
for.

## The eight pages
1. Cover: title, DiGi, three sample fish, ages 2 to 5, print and play badges.
2. How to play plus a warm grown up note (colour is the first sorting skill),
   with a strip of the six named colours.
3 to 4. The six colour fishbowls, three big bowls a page, each one clear colour
   with a named colour chip: red, orange, yellow / green, blue, pink.
5 to 6. Cut out fish sheets, four chunky big eyed fish per colour in dashed
   cut cells, three colours a page.
7. Colour and match: six blank white fish outlines, each labelled with the
   colour to use, for the child to colour then match.
8. Funnel page: the free Shapes and Colours Splash Pack at
   guidedchildhood.com/etsy-bonus, plus a warm review ask.
9 (colour in only). DiGi's colouring tips.

## The six colours
Red #E23B3B, Orange #D4600A (house coral), Yellow #EAB308, Green #2F8F6B
(house green), Blue #2F7FC9, Pink #E85FA0. Chosen to be genuinely distinct for
a toddler matching game. Each fish and its bowl share the colour so matching
works. Red is not a standard house token but a colour matching game needs a
true red, so it is defined only inside this product.

## Rebuild
From the scratchpad: `node gen-fish.mjs` writes print.html, then
`node build-fish.mjs` renders all three PDFs and both preview sets (Playwright,
chromium at /opt/pw-browsers). Edit gen-fish.mjs (colours, copy, layout) then
rerun both.
