# DiGi's A to Z Showdown — product folder

Nearest recipe: escape and detective kits (printables-engine recipe 4) for the
anatomy of a boxed game night, priced at the entry rung to open the games
ladder, plus the La Casita gap 1 finding: a print and play alphabet wheel quiz
has zero English language competition on Etsy (verified July 2026 sweep).
Fronted by DiGi, the whole family lane. Evergreen.

Built 21 July 2026 by the printables-engine pipeline. Inspired by the classic
letter wheel quiz format, but the name, the wheel design and all 78 clues are
entirely our own; no TV format is named or imitated anywhere.

## Contents
- `product/` — a-to-z-showdown-A4.pdf (colour), USLetter.pdf,
  A4-ink-friendly.pdf. Nine pages: cover, how to play, the letter wheel,
  three rounds of 26 clue cards (Starter 7 to 9, Challenger 9 to 11, Family
  Showdown), score sheet, write your own round, free bonus page.
- `phone/` — 1080x1920 send to child phone set: the wheel screen and the how
  to play screen.
- `images/` — DiGi-star.svg, page previews, and the imagery ledger (images.md).
- `listing/` — full Etsy listing package: title, tags, photo plan, video and
  reel scripts, compliance notes.
- `print.html`, `phone.html`, `fonts/` — the editable source. The wheel and
  all clue card grids are generated by inline JS (trigonometry for the 26
  badge positions) that runs before print. Rebuild with the Playwright script
  referenced in images.md.
