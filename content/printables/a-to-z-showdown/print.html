Two minutes before you play

How to play, in five easy steps

1

Set the table. Print the wheel, cut out the round one cards, and stack them A to Z. One player is the host and holds the cards. Everyone else races.

2

Read the clue. The host starts at A and reads the clue aloud. Every answer begins with the letter on the card. X is the sneaky one: its answer just has an X hiding inside.

3

Colour the wheel. Right answer: colour that letter green. Wrong answer: colour it coral. Stuck? Say pass, and that letter waits for you at the end of the lap.

4

Finish the lap. Keep circling the wheel until every letter is green or coral. Passed letters come back round in order, as many laps as it takes.

5

Count the greens. That is your score out of 26. Write it on the score sheet, then swap hosts for the next round.

Way one · Solo

Beat the clock. Set a timer for six minutes and race the wheel alone. Tomorrow, race your own record. The wheel never gets tired of a rematch.

Way two · Versus

Beat your sibling. Take turns, one letter each, or print a wheel per player and race the same clues. Most greens wins. Best of three rounds takes the crown.

Host tips from me, DiGi! Read every clue twice, nice and slow. Never mouth the answer. Cheer the passes too, because a pass is just a letter saving its moment.
