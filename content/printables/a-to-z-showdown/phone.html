DiGi's A to Z Showdown

How to play

1

Set the table. Wheel out flat, clue cards stacked A to Z, one player hosts.

2

Read the clue. Every answer begins with the letter on the card. X just hides inside its answer.

3

Colour the wheel. Right is green, wrong is coral, and a pass waits for the next lap.

4

Finish the lap. Keep circling until every letter has a colour.

5

Count the greens. That is your score out of 26. Best of three rounds takes the crown.

Read every clue twice, nice and slow. Never mouth the answer. And cheer the passes, they always come back round!
