Word Fishing

How to play

the
1

Catch and say

Catch a fish, read the word out loud. Read it right and it goes in your bucket. Most fish wins.

2

Word match

The grown up calls a word. Fish until you land that exact word. Reading with a mission.

3

Beat the clock

Two minutes on the timer. How many fish can you catch and read? Beat your own best tomorrow.

Keep it playful, never a test. Stuck on a word? Say it together, throw the fish back, play on.
