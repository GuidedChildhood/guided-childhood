## What is inside (listing description bullets)
- A print and play jobs game fronted by Zara the Truth Finder: clap the
  syllables, cut the tiles and build the word, then match every worker to
  their place and their tool
- Eight UK jobs your child will know: baker, doctor, farmer, teacher, builder,
  vet, chef and postie
- Cut out syllable tiles like BA and KER, push them together and build the
  whole word in the letter boxes
- A clap the syllables page that shows how many beats are in each job
- A big matching page that brings all eight workers and tools together to solve
- A grown up guide with how to play and a full answer key
- Two ways to print: a full colour edition and a colour in line art edition
  your child can bring to life themselves
- A bonus colouring tips page from Zara in the colour in edition
- A4 and US Letter sizes, print as many times as you like, laminate and reuse
