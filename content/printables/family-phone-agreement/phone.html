When something feels wrong
Tummy drop moment? You do not have to fix it. Just three steps.
1
Stop
Put the phone down. No replies, no clicks, no arguing back.
2
Keep it
Screenshot if you can. Forward it to nobody. Delete nothing. It is proof, not your fault.
3
Tell your adult
Today, not someday. They signed a promise to stay calm, remember?
You will never be in trouble for telling.
Not even if you clicked something. Telling is always the winning move.
