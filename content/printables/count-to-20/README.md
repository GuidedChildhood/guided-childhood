# Count to 20 with Sofia

A print and play early numbers set for ages 4 to 7, fronted by Sofia the Safety Guardian. Replicates the La Casita Educativa "Learn the numbers 1 to 20" format (number cards plus a counting manipulative) in the Guided Childhood house style.

## Recipe
- **Type**: Early learning cards (recipe 5, animal and squad lane), extended with a ten frame manipulative and count and match activity.
- **Age band**: 4 to 7.
- **Occasion**: Evergreen, lifts at back to school and new term.
- **Character**: Sofia (Safety Guardian, ages 4 to 9, colour green). Warm, brave, gentle voice.
- **Price rung**: £3.50.

## Pages (9)
1. Cover, Sofia, "Count to 20 with me", ages 4 to 7
2. How to play plus grown up guide (one to one counting, number to amount link)
3 to 5. Number cards 1 to 20 to cut out, big numeral plus matching dots panel (rows of five)
6. Ten frame counting mat, two ten frames for counters
7. Count and match, five groups of pictures to match to number cards
8. Colour by number mini page (Sofia in her garden)
9. Free bonus and review funnel

Colour in edition adds page 10, Sofia's colouring tips.

## Files
- `print.html` — single source, all pages. Number cards, ten frames and count and match groups are generated by inline JS so every quantity is guaranteed to equal its numeral.
- `product/count-to-20-colour-A4.pdf` — full colour, A4
- `product/count-to-20-colour-USLetter.pdf` — full colour, US Letter
- `product/count-to-20-colour-in-A4.pdf` — bold colourable line art via the `colourin` body class, includes the tips page
- `images/` — Sofia.jpeg plus preview-page-N.png (colour) and colour-in-preview-page-N.png
- `listing/listing.md` — full Etsy package
- `fonts/` — local Nunito and IBM Plex Mono (self contained, no network at print time)

## House rules applied
- Nunito plus IBM Plex Mono, cream and ink tokens, chunky 2.5px ink cards, 16px radius, 0 5px 0 ink shadow.
- Sofia's colour is green. Real art on the cover and speech bubble avatar, hand drawn line art elsewhere and as the colour in fallback.
- No dashes anywhere. UK spelling. Ages written "4 to 7".
- Discreet guidedchildhood.com footer on every page. Bonus gated at guidedchildhood.com/etsy-bonus. No affiliate links, no medical claims, no off Etsy checkout.
