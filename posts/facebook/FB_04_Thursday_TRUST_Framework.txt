Five letters. One framework. Every stage from 4 to 16.

T — Timing. Right device at the right age. Not all-or-nothing.
R — Relationships. Connection is the strongest protective factor the research has found.
U — Upstream. Environment before rules. Platform mechanics matter more than willpower.
S — Sleep. The bedroom rule is the single highest-impact action in this system.
T — Transparency. Openness over secrecy. Co-navigation over monitoring.

This is the TRUST framework. It runs through every lesson, every script, and every week of the Guided Childhood platform.

Built on the research of Odgers, Orben, Przybylski, and Livingstone — four of the leading researchers in child digital development.

Not a tip. Not a rule. A framework.
