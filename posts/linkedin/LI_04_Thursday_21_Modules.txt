21 half-term modules. EYFS to Year 13.

Here's what the Guided Childhood school curriculum covers:

EYFS/KS1: Screens and kindness. Real vs not real. Shared viewing habits.
KS2: Algorithms, privacy basics, internet safety, coding foundations.
KS3: Social media mechanics, AI chatbots, deepfakes, dark web awareness.
KS4: Manipulation, sextortion, radicalisation, preparation for full digital independence.
KS5: AI mastery, data rights, vibe coding, emerging technology literacy.

Every lesson includes: teacher plan, pupil worksheet, slides, and a parent note.

Fully mapped to:
— Online Safety Act 2023
— Statutory RSE and Health Education
— Education for a Connected World (DfE/UKCIS)
— Ofcom Media Literacy Framework
— DfE AI in Education Guidance (June 2025)
