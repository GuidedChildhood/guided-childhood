The five questions a doctor would ask.

Whether you have noticed something that concerns you, or you simply want to understand your child's digital world better, these are the things a doctor would want to know first. Not screen time. Not which apps. These.

Five questions. Under a minute. Some changes in sleep and mood are part of normal adolescent development — this check helps you tell the difference. There are no right answers, only honest ones.
