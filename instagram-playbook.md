## SECTION 5: DELIVERY GUIDE — HOW TO FILM

### The Setup (Dr. Becky method — proven at 3.4M followers)
- Phone held at eye level in selfie mode, OR on a basic stand
- Plain wall behind you — your consistent background, every video
- Natural light from a window facing you, or a ring light
- Neat, consistent appearance — no heavy styling needed
- No editing. One take. Rawness is a feature, not a flaw.

### The 5 Delivery Principles
1. **Eye contact into the lens** the entire time. Not the screen — the lens. This is your trust signal.
2. **Slow down on your hook.** The first sentence must land. Pause after it.
3. **Speed up slightly in the body.** Dense, fast delivery signals value.
4. **Slow down for the payoff.** The revelation should be slower than everything before it.
5. **Lean forward slightly** when you say something important. It mimics urgency.

### On-Screen Text — CRITICAL
85% of Instagram videos are watched on mute. Add a text overlay of your hook line in the first 2 seconds.
- Bold white text, black shadow, centred
- One line only — do not block your face
- Free apps: CapCut, Splice, InShot

### What NOT to Do
- Never open with "Hey everyone, today I want to talk about..."
- Never add an intro card or logo
- Never apologise for video quality
- Never read from notes — know your 3 key points before filming
- Never explain your credentials in the hook — the depth of your knowledge does that work

## SECTION 9: THE ALGORITHM RULES (apply to every post)

| Signal | Weight | How to engineer it |
|---|---|---|
| DM Sends | Highest | Make content parents forward to their co-parent tonight |
| Saves | Very high | Give scripts, frameworks, and evidence worth bookmarking |
| Watch time | High | Hook must hold 60%+ past 3 seconds — start mid-tension |
| Comments | Medium | End with one specific question |
| Likes | Lowest | Do not optimise for this |

**The DM test:** Before posting anything, ask — "Would a parent DM this to their partner right now?" If yes, post it. If not, rework the hook or payoff.

**The save test:** "Would a parent bookmark this to refer back to?" If yes, the saves will come.

**Posting frequency:** 3–5 Reels per week + daily Stories. Consistency for 90 days beats any single viral moment.
