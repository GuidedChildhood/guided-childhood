Before you complete this form

  • Do not promise confidentiality to a pupil before, during, or after a disclosure.
  • Listen carefully, thank the pupil for sharing, and do not press for additional detail.
  • Record what was said in the pupil's own words where possible — do not interpret or paraphrase.
  • Submit this form to the DSL before the end of the school day. If urgent, speak to the DSL immediately.
  • Do not investigate the concern yourself or speak to parents before consulting the DSL.
