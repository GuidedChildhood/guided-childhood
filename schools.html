Complete Scheme of Work

21 half-term modules from EYFS to Year 13. Mapped to UK Year Group, Key Stage, and every statutory framework. Two layers in every stage: behaviour and routines first, digital literacy second.

  • EYFS & KS1 (Yr R–2): screens and kindness, real vs not real, co-viewing habits
  • KS2 (Yr 3–6): screen routines, gaming time, boredom and screens, algorithms and privacy
  • KS3 (Yr 7–9): mood and screens, social media, group chats, workarounds, deepfakes
  • KS4 (Yr 10–11): manipulation, sextortion, radicalisation, readiness for 16
  • KS5 (Yr 12–13): AI mastery, data rights, vibe coding, future technology
  • Every lesson: teacher plan, pupil worksheet, slides, parent note

What the curriculum actually covers

The research does not separate screen time from behaviour. Neither does this curriculum. Every module addresses the full picture — from the daily routines children struggle with at home, to the digital literacy skills they need as they grow.
