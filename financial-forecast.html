TopicDetail

Not modelled — upside only NHS / LA partnerships · International revenue Years 1–2 · White-label licensing · MAT framework agreements · Viral growth events
Policy catalyst UK social media ban modelled as a demand spike in Q3–Q4 Year 1. Even if delayed, the base case shows profitability in Year 2 without it.
Use of the £250k raise Marketing (£90k) · Schools partnerships (£65k) · Product development (£55k) · Operations + founder salary (£40k). This is not a fundraise to cover losses.
Exit potential At £1M+ ARR with established school relationships, the business is attractive to educational publishers, school platform providers, and parenting media groups. Series A or strategic acquisition in Years 4–5.
