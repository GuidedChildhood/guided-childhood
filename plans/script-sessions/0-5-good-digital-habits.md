# Script session: Good digital habits from the start (0 to 5)

**Band:** 0 to 5 (parent only, the child never watches)
**Module:** gap. Nothing in the 21 modules covers this band; this session helps define the new 0 to 5 parent only strand named in plans/parent-video-research-2026-07.md.
**Cast:** DiGi only (golden star). No child track at this band.
**Single action outcome for the parent:** I can put screens into our routine on purpose and join in, instead of letting the routine build itself.

---

## Track P — the parent session (60 to 90 seconds)

**P-BEAT 1 — THE MOMENT (dialogue beat, 12s)**
It is five o'clock. Dinner will not cook itself, so the tablet goes on. Same sofa, same show, same small pang of guilt. Here is the thing: nobody in your house ever decided this was the routine. It built itself.

**P-BEAT 2 — THE REFRAME (concept beat, 30s)**
Here is the pathway view. At this age, habits are not about willpower, yours or theirs. They are about architecture. Your child is not forming bad habits; the routine is simply doing what routines do, repeating whatever landed there first. So the move is not less screen or no screen. It is placed screen: a slot you chose, with a clear start, a clear end, and you in it sometimes. That last part matters most, because watching together turns a babysitter into a conversation. And the research is on your side here: a 2025 review in JMIR Pediatrics found that when parents build knowledge and skills around screens, screen time came down for parents and children both. The habit you shape now is the one they carry.

**P-BEAT 3 — TRY THIS TONIGHT (mission beat, 20s)**
One move, two parts. Keep the five o'clock show exactly where it is, and change how it starts and ends. First, sit in for the first five minutes and talk about what you see. Borrow this: ooh, he shared his tractor, did you see that? Second, end it with the same words every single time: when this one finishes, the TV goes to sleep. Same slot, same closing line, you in the picture. That is not screen time happening to your family anymore. That is a habit you built on purpose.

**P-BEAT 4 — DIGI TAG (5s, from the DiGi library)**
DiGi star wave: Want help shaping your family's screen routine? Tell me what your five o'clock looks like and we will build it together.
