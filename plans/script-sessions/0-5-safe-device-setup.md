# Script session: Safe device setup and parental controls (0 to 5)

**Band:** 0 to 5 (parent only, the child never watches)
**Module:** gap. Nothing in the 21 modules covers this band; this session helps define the new 0 to 5 parent only strand named in plans/parent-video-research-2026-07.md.
**Cast:** DiGi only (golden star). No child track at this band.
**Single action outcome for the parent:** I can set the device up before I hand it over, so the fence does the supervising when I cannot.

---

## Track P — the parent session (60 to 90 seconds)

**P-BEAT 1 — THE MOMENT (dialogue beat, 12s)**
Your three year old is in the trolley seat watching nursery rhymes on your phone. You look away to find the pasta. Two swipes and one autoplay later, the phone is playing something you have never seen, with sounds you do not like. She did nothing wrong. She just swiped.

**P-BEAT 2 — THE REFRAME (concept beat, 30s)**
Here is the pathway view. This is not a trust problem, and it is not proof that screens are dangerous. It is a setup problem. Your phone arrived configured for an adult, and a three year old holding an adult's phone is a toddler in an unfenced garden. At this age children do not need rules and warnings; they cannot hold them yet. They need a fence, built once, by you. Every phone and tablet already has one waiting: a child profile or kids mode, downloads and purchases locked behind your passcode, autoplay switched off. Ten minutes of setup replaces a thousand moments of hovering, and it means the device your child holds is a device that was actually meant for them.

**P-BEAT 3 — TRY THIS TONIGHT (mission beat, 20s)**
One move, tonight after bedtime. Take the device your child uses most and build the fence: switch on its child profile or kids mode, put downloads and purchases behind your passcode, and turn autoplay off. Then tomorrow, hand it over with this: this is your bit of the tablet. Everything in here is for you. From then on you are not watching over their shoulder. The fence is.

**P-BEAT 4 — DIGI TAG (5s, from the DiGi library)**
DiGi star wave: Stuck on a settings screen? Tell me which device you have and we will walk through it together.

---

## Track C — the child session

None at this band. The child never watches. Per the banding table in plans/video-script-sessions.md, 0 to 5 is parent only; setup happens after bedtime with the device in the parent's hands, so no child facing moment is needed. The parent IS the lesson.
