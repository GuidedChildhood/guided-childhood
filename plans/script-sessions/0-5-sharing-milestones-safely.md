# Script session: Sharing milestone moments safely (0 to 5)

**Band:** 0 to 5 (parent only, the child never watches)
**Module:** gap. Nothing in the 21 modules covers this band; this session helps define the new 0 to 5 parent only strand named in plans/parent-video-research-2026-07.md.
**Cast:** DiGi only (golden star). No child track at this band.
**Single action outcome for the parent:** I can share the proud moments after two quick checks, so my child's story stays theirs. This one is about my posting, not my child's screen.

---

## Track P — the parent session (60 to 90 seconds)

**P-BEAT 1 — THE MOMENT (dialogue beat, 12s)**
First morning of nursery. The uniform is slightly too big, the smile is enormous, and the doorstep photo is perfect. Your thumb is hovering over share, because Grandma is two hundred miles away and this is how she gets to be there.

**P-BEAT 2 — THE REFRAME (concept beat, 30s)**
Here is the pathway view, and it starts with this: sharing is not the problem. Posting the proud moments is how modern families love out loud, and nobody should feel guilty for it. But your child is building a public record before they can say the word public, and right now you are its editor. So the move is not never post. It is two questions before you post. One: who exactly can see this? Not roughly. Exactly. Two: would my child be glad this exists when they are fourteen? And know the two posts that give away the most: birthdays and first days of school. Between them they can hand a stranger a full name, a date of birth and a school. Share the joy. Keep those details out of shot.

**P-BEAT 3 — TRY THIS TONIGHT (mission beat, 20s)**
One move tonight. Open the app you post on most and look at who can actually see your posts. If the answer is public or friends of friends, either tighten it or move the milestone photos to the family group chat instead. And if a relative asks why, borrow this: we are keeping the little one's photos in the family group now, so this one is just for us. Nobody misses a moment, and nothing about your child sits where a stranger can find it.

**P-BEAT 4 — DIGI TAG (5s, from the DiGi library)**
DiGi star wave: Wondering about a photo before you post it? Describe it to me and we will run the two checks together.
