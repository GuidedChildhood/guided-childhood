# How it all hinges together, the tidy and flow map

20 July 2026. The justification behind the simplified flow, written before the
change lands so the intent survives the build. Nothing here dismantles what is
built. Every feature keeps its engine; only its place and size on the screen
change.

## The one loop that everything serves

The product is one loop, and every feature we have built is a gear in it:

1. **Real world jobs earn stars.** Family quests, ticked by the child in their
   own app from the right age, approved by the parent.
2. **Stars buy screen time through the timer.** Phone, tablet, TV and console
   all run through the same countdown, visible to both sides. The healthy age
   guide winds a session up on its own, anything past it is a treat the parent
   knowingly gives. That is the calibrated pathway, never allow or deny.
3. **The parent invests ten minutes a day.** Check in, moment, script, DiGi.
   The daily path is the habit spine, same shape every day.
4. **Everything reports into the four ticks.** Safe online reads device setup
   plus worries plus stage lessons plus DiGi's weekly question. Healthy
   balance reads the timer against the jobs. AI and social open at the age the
   research says, and are earned through the stage lessons.
5. **Ticks earn passport stamps, stamps walk the road to 16.** One road
   language everywhere: parent pathway, home strip, DiGi welcome, child app.
6. **DiGi sees the whole loop.** The brain carries the family memory, the
   concerns ledger, what worked, the tracker, the agreement, the timer's own
   numbers and the expert knowledge, so a question about a Switch gets
   answered from this family's actual setup and gently points at the missing
   gear (the family deal, the timer) when one is not in place.
7. **Sunday closes the week.** The round up reads the same numbers the ticks
   read, and DiGi's graded questions feed forward into next week's ticks.

The homepage sells exactly this loop to a parent who has not joined yet:
problem, the third way, the loop as how we help, the four ticks as what you
get, the road as where it leads.

## The tidy and flow rules, with the research behind them

**One next action, rendered big.** Duolingo's home is a path of fat nodes with
one callout riding on the current one. Our daily ten minutes renders the same
way: the engine (TodayPathStrip's steps and minute budget) is untouched, only
drawn at Duolingo size. Justification: a parent deciding what to do is a
parent who might do nothing. The screen decides for them.

**Live family state pops up, it does not squat.** GoHenry's parent home leads
with the child's live money state. Ours does the same with the timer: when a
session is running, a live chip with the countdown sits on the parent home and
the child's screen owns the same countdown. When nothing is running, it stays
out of the way. Pop up on relevance, disappear on rest.

**Big icon selects.** Folded rows and pick lists use large icon tiles, chunky
butter and ink, the pressed 0 5px 0 edge. A tap target the size of a thumb is
a courtesy on a phone held one handed at bedtime.

**Same shape every day.** The home screen never rearranges itself. Sunday adds
exactly one row, the round up. Habit lives in sameness; novelty lives inside
the content, not the layout.

**DiGi speaks first.** Good Inside's pattern: the guide greets before the
dashboard asks anything. One sentence, road position folded in, then the path.

**The road is the hero where the road is the point.** On the pathway page the
road gets Duolingo scale: full width, 72 to 88px stamps, sticky position
header. On the home it is one slim row, because the home's point is today.

## Where everything lives after the tidy

| Piece | Before | After |
|---|---|---|
| Daily ten minutes | Small horizontal stepper card | Big vertical path, centre of home |
| Timer | Card on quests page | Same card, plus live pop up chip on home when running |
| Family quests | Cards on home and quests page | Slim row on home with approve badge, full board on quests page |
| Road to 16 | Strip on home, medium road on pathway | Slim row on home, hero road on pathway |
| Literacy ticks | Tracker page | Tracker page, unchanged |
| Goal cards | Home | Quests page |
| Weekly round up | Own page, Sunday | Own page, plus one Sunday row on home |
| DiGi | Everywhere | Everywhere, greeting first on home, brain untouched |
| Kid handover QR | Buried | Surfaces on the quests row at the 8 to 10 band when no kid link exists |

Nothing is deleted. Every engine keeps running exactly as programmed.
