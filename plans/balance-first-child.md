# Balance first on the child app

Written 2026-07-18 from Justin's direction. The child app should celebrate the
right thing. Screen time is the payoff, never the hero. What we reward and
celebrate is the balance: real world jobs, school, housework and connection on
one side, a healthy amount of screen on the other, and the streak of showing up.

## The principle
We never push device use. We push jobs and offline activity, and we celebrate
when the balance between the two is healthy. Less screen and more doing is the
win, and there is real science behind it (self determination theory: competence,
autonomy and relatedness come from doing and connecting, not from consuming;
effort before reward builds delay tolerance; offline activity and outdoor time
protect sleep, mood and attention). Every nudge and celebration leans that way.

## Shipped now (this PR)
- The three stacked cards (star bank, today's screen time, use my device time)
  are one clear balance card.
- The hero line is the balance we celebrate, not the minutes: a warm well done
  when jobs earned are at or above screen used, a gentle nudge to do a job when
  screen has run ahead.
- The job streak is celebrated on the card (flame + day count).
- Device time is folded behind the card and opens on a tap, so screen is never
  the front and centre thing.

## Guardrails
- Never shame screen use. The framing is always add a job, not stop watching.
- No dashes in any copy.
- Grounded in the science bank, in Justin's warm plain voice.
- Nudges capped so they land as encouragement, never nagging.
