# Starter pack result screen: the wow page spec

**Source:** Justin, 3 Jul late. The questions flow is approved. The result page must become the best product entry page anywhere: immersive, animated, Apple finish, Duolingo journey energy, Good Inside warmth.

## The journey the page tells (in order, each beat animating in)
1. **Reassure and name it** (keep): dark "Sound familiar?" card + the stage card, pastel stage colour.
2. **Your first week with us**: a day by day animated strip, Duolingo path style. Day 1: a moment card (the 5pm meltdown) and the exact script. Day 2: the tracker, one tap. Day 3: DiGi at 11pm with a real answer. Every day: five minutes. Friday: the check in that plans next week. Each day a pastel card (rotate stage-1 to stage-5 tints) sliding in along a dotted path with DiGi moving along it (GSAP scrub or staggered reveal).
3. **What you get flashes**: quick confident feature cards: 100 plus scripts, the wellbeing tracker with streaks, DiGi always on, monthly lessons and digital homeschooling (flash 2 or 3 lesson slide thumbnails), family agreement, school message reminders (never miss a PE kit day, a scouts trip, lunch money: label this one Coming soon), weekly advice emails written by a parent not a robot.
4. **The pathway simulation**: the five stage dotted pathway 4 to 16 with DiGi walking it as you scroll, each stage dot lighting its pastel.
5. **Save CTA** (keep the dark card): create the free account, pathway saved, no card required.

## Rules
- Pastels from globals.css tokens only (stage-1 to stage-5 + bold variants), butter #EDC35F reserved for CTAs and highlights, no yellow text on cream.
- Nunito display, IBM Plex Mono eyebrows. No dashes in any copy. Justin's voice: warm, plain, direct.
- GSAP only. Sequential reveals, 0.5 to 0.7s, gentle. One CTA visible at rest.
- This page shows once per visitor (it is the quiz result), so it can afford theatre. Mobile first: check 390px and 1440px.
- Higgsfield intro video: leave a commented slot for an 8s DiGi welcome clip, do not block on it.

## Quality bar
Iterate with a critique pass against: Apple (calm confidence, one idea per screen), Duolingo (journey, streaks, delight), Good Inside (warmth, reassurance). Do not ship the first draft.
