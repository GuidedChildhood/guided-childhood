NOW

LinkedIn — Post Today

  • Choose Option A (cliff edge) or Option B (phone is not banned) and post now. The ban conversation is happening on LinkedIn today.
    • OPTION A — The cliff edge Today the UK government confirmed it. Under-16s off social media. Regulation before Christmas. Ban in force Spring 2027. I want to tell you what nobody else is saying today. The children who comply will hit 16 on the day the ban lifts with no graduated exposure, no framework, and no preparation for what they are about to access. The switch flips. Two years of being kept away from it. Then suddenly all of it, at once. That is the cliff edge the ban creates. The children who do not comply will navigate around it in darker, less monitored spaces. Without the minimal safeguards the platforms provide. Without the conversations that should have happened first. Neither group is ready. The ban is not the education. Spring 2027 is the deadline. That is what the next nine months are for. Justin Phillips, Guided Childhood
    OPTION B — The phone is not banned Something important is being lost in today's announcement. The government has banned social media for under-16s. Not smartphones. Your child will still have a phone. They will still have WhatsApp. They will still have gaming. They will still encounter algorithms. They will still be in group chats. They will still see things that are hard to talk about. The ban removes Instagram and TikTok. It does not remove the need for the conversation. If anything, it makes it more urgent. Because from Spring 2027, every difficult conversation that used to happen gradually, inside a platform, now happens in one go when a 16-year-old gets legal access for the first time. The preparation starts now. Justin Phillips, Guided Childhood
    STEP 1

    Supabase — Database

    • Go to supabase.com and create a new project
    • Open the SQL editor and run the full schema from guided-childhood-build/docs/02
    • Copy these three values from Supabase → Settings → API:NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL · NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY · SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY
    STEP 2

    Stripe — Payments

    • Go to stripe.com — switch to test mode first
    • Create product: Founder rate — £7.99/month recurringCopy the Price ID (starts price_) → STRIPE_FOUNDER_PRICE_ID
    • Create product: Standard — £12.99/month recurringCopy the Price ID → STRIPE_STANDARD_PRICE_ID
    • Create product: Annual — £99/year recurringCopy the Price ID → STRIPE_ANNUAL_PRICE_ID
    • Copy your Stripe secret key and publishable keySTRIPE_SECRET_KEY (starts sk_test_) · NEXT_PUBLIC_STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY (starts pk_test_)
    STEP 3

    Vercel — Environment Variables

    • Go to Vercel project → Settings → Environment Variables
    • Add all variables from .env.local.template in the codebase
    NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_URL = from Supabase step 1
    NEXT_PUBLIC_SUPABASE_ANON_KEY = from Supabase step 1
    SUPABASE_SERVICE_ROLE_KEY = from Supabase step 1
    STRIPE_SECRET_KEY = from Stripe step 2
    NEXT_PUBLIC_STRIPE_PUBLISHABLE_KEY = from Stripe step 2
    STRIPE_FOUNDER_PRICE_ID = from Stripe step 2
    STRIPE_STANDARD_PRICE_ID = from Stripe step 2
    STRIPE_ANNUAL_PRICE_ID = from Stripe step 2
    STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET = from Stripe step 4
    DIGI_MODEL = claude-fable-5
    ANTHROPIC_API_KEY = from console.anthropic.com
    STEP 4

    Stripe Webhook

    • In Stripe → Developers → Webhooks → Add endpoint
    • Endpoint URL: https://your-vercel-domain.vercel.app/api/stripe/webhook
    • Events to listen for:checkout.session.completed · customer.subscription.updated · customer.subscription.deleted · invoice.payment_failed
    • Copy the webhook signing secret → add as STRIPE_WEBHOOK_SECRET in Vercel
    STEP 5

    App Icons (PWA)

    • Create public/icons/icon-192.png — 192×192px, Guided Childhood logo on cream background
    • Create public/icons/icon-512.png — 512×512px, same design
    • Commit and push both files to the branch
    STEP 6

    Deploy and Test

    • Vercel will auto-deploy once all env vars are set. Or trigger manually: Vercel → Deployments → Redeploy
    • Test: visit /starter-pack, complete the quiz, sign up, complete onboarding
    • Test: visit /dashboard — stage card shows correctly
    • Test: send a DiGi message — response arrives
    • Test: visit /dashboard/upgrade — founder count shows from Supabase
    • Test: mobile view in Chrome DevTools — all pages responsive
