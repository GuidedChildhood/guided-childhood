# Character system decision: DiGi and the Sparks, upgraded

Written 22 July 2026. Feeds the character build. Builds on
plans/character-maturation-plan.md and plans/physical-rewards-plan.md. Backed by
a three way research sweep (viral kids IP, mascot retention plus design theory,
and our own platform fit). No dashes in any copy, per brand rule.

## The decision

Keep DiGi as the golden star, unchanged, and build the five Sparks around it,
one per stage. Take the Sparks we already committed to and sharpen them with the
mechanics the research proved, folding in the two ideas raised this week (the
constellation reveal and the level up feeling) without fighting our own canon.

## The four candidates, and why the Sparks win

| Candidate | Fits DiGi canon | One character growing up reads clean | Retention fit | Viral and merch fit | Verdict |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Maturing Sparks (star family) | Yes, star DNA on each | Yes, baby to grown, one family | Strong | Charms, cards, pins already planned | Winner |
| Constellation animals | Partial | No, a hedgehog does not grow into a fox | Strong | Strong | Fold the mechanic in, drop mixed species |
| Evolving robots | No, the robot is retired canon | Yes | Strong | Fair | Out |
| Star point build up | Yes, closest to the star | Neutral | Weak | Weak | Fold in as the payoff, not the cast |

Robots are out because DiGi was once a robot and we retired it, so the concept
fights our single strongest brand constraint. Mixed species animals are out
because five different animals read as five characters, not one child growing up,
which is the exact objection that already demoted the UK animal guides.

## The evidence in short

Viral with kids comes from round huggable shapes, a one stroke silhouette that
reads at icon size, big expressive eyes, a name and a backstory each, and a
family you complete (Labubu, Squishmallows, Pokemon, Duo). Blind box surprise
works but its buyers are adults, so we borrow the collect the set thrill, never
paid randomness, which would break our non negotiables.

What actually retains is one mechanic above all: the character state IS the
child progress (Finch the bird, Habitica the pets, Forest the tree). Care plus
forgiveness, a home screen widget, and gentle re engagement stack on top. Guilt
heavy streaks suit Duolingo adults, not our children.

Design theory: round equals safe, a big head and eyes equals care for me, and it
must survive shrinking to 16 pixels. Evolve the form and the depth of
interaction as the child grows while keeping the silhouette and eyes constant,
so it never ages out from 4 to 16.

## What upgraded means, concretely

1. The constellation becomes the shelf. An unearned Spark shows as a faint
   constellation outline, and doing the real tasks fills it in and resolves it
   into the finished Spark. The outline to friend reveal, kept as the one star
   family rather than mixed animals.
2. The level up becomes the wake up. Each Spark starts sleepy and faint at 0
   percent and powers up to full colour with its prop at 100 percent as the
   child does the work.
3. The Spark state is the child progress, tied to the daily 10 minutes, with our
   existing no guilt forgiveness so a missed day never breaks the bond.
4. Complete all five and you rebuild DiGi whole star, and unlock the hidden
   golden baby DiGi already specced as the earned surprise.
5. Every Spark carries DiGi gold star mark, the same eyes and smile, one stage
   colour, one skill prop. Names and one line backstories give the attachment,
   and the simple silhouette makes them merch ready for the charms, cards and
   stickers in the rewards plan.
6. Add a home screen widget of the current Spark, the highest retention lever
   after growth itself.

## The five Sparks

One per stage, youngest to oldest, each maturing down a baby schema gradient
(head to body ratio shrinks, palette deepens, the prop grows up), all carrying
DiGi star mark.

| Spark | Stage | Ages | Colour | Skill it carries |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pip | Foundation | 4 to 7 | butter yellow | steady stops, stop and tell |
| Glim | Builder | 8 to 10 | sky blue | healthy habits, how it works |
| Vela | Explorer | 11 to 13 | rose | the feed, comparison, the highlight reel |
| Ember | Shaper | 13 to 15 | pink | settings, footprint, the honest check |
| Nova | Independent | 16 plus | lavender | readiness, taking the wheel |
