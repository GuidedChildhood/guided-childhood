## Getting real testers

- Recruit 10 to 20 families from your network, a parenting group, and a school contact.
- Give them the founder rate, free or discounted, in exchange for honest feedback.
- Send a two minute Loom of the first run, or do a short onboarding call.
- One feedback channel: a simple form or a WhatsApp group.
- Success signals to watch in the first week: they finish setup, they come back on day 2, they send DiGi at least one message, they set up quests, they read one script.
