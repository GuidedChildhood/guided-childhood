## How to start, and where to go

Here is the actual first hour, click by click.

**Step 1. Open your one sheet.** The tracking sheet from Part 1 of the playbook. Add a second tab called Creators with these columns: handle, platform, followers, why they fit, email or DM, date sent, reply, booked, video date.

**Step 2. Go find twenty of them.** You are looking for UK parenting creators, 10k to 250k followers, who already post real family life and have touched the phone or screen topic. Two places:

- **Instagram.** In the search bar try these tags and scroll the top posts: `#ukmum` `#mumsofinstagram` `#parentingteens` `#dadlife` `#screentimebattle` `#gentleparentinguk`. When you find one that fits, look at who Instagram suggests next (the similar accounts arrow on their profile), that is the fastest way to find ten more like them.
- **TikTok.** Search the same words without the hash: uk mum day in the life, parenting teens, screen time. Filter your eye to UK accounts, the audience has to be British for your signups to be British.

**Step 3. Vet each one in ten seconds.** Before you add them to the sheet, check three things. Does their content actually show real family life, not just polished ads. Do the comments show a real engaged audience, not just likes. Are they and their followers UK based. If yes to all three, add them to the Creators tab and note why they fit.

**Step 4. Find how to contact them.** On their profile, look for a collab or business email, usually in the bio or behind the Email button on a professional account. If there is an email, that is your first choice, use Message 1 there. If not, use the DM. Note which in the sheet.

**Step 5. Send Message 1 to all twenty.** One sitting. Personalise the [name] and the one real detail every time, that single specific line is what gets a reply. Log the date sent next to each.

**Step 6. Expect a slow start, this is normal.** Jake said the opening is tough and you get very few replies. That is the job, not a failure. Twenty DMs might get you three or four real conversations, and that is enough to book two or three videos.

**Step 7. When someone replies, send Message 3, then Message 4 once booked.** Keep the total spend inside your cap. Two or three videos is a complete first test.

**Step 8. When a video goes live, watch your sheet for 48 hours.** A jump in signups on the day a video lands is the win. Keep that creator, drop the ones who did not move the line, and only then spend more.

**Tools you do not need yet.** Jake used Koroi for creator payments and Sideshift for sourcing. You do not need either to run three creators. A sheet, an email, and a bank transfer is the whole system for now. Add tools only when you outgrow the sheet.

**The very first thing to do right now:** open the sheet, make the Creators tab, and find your first five names on Instagram. That is the start.
