How to view the investor documents

All documents are hosted live at guidedchildhood.com. Open any URL below in Chrome for the best experience. The pitch deck runs as an interactive presentation directly in your browser.

Viewing the pitch deck as slides
1
Open guidedchildhood.com/investor-deck in Chrome on a laptop or desktop.
2
Press F11 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+F (Mac) to go fullscreen.
3
Use ← → arrow keys to move between slides, or swipe on a touchscreen.
4
Press Esc to exit fullscreen. Use the dots at the bottom to jump to any slide.
Saving any document as a PDF
1
Open the URL in Chrome. Use the live URL, not a downloaded file.
2
Press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac) to open the print dialog.
3
Set destination to Save as PDF. Margins: None. Tick Background graphics.
4
For the deck, use guidedchildhood.com/investor-deck-print (all slides on one page).
Important: Always print from the live URL, not from a saved HTML file. This ensures fonts load correctly and the PDF looks identical to the browser view.
