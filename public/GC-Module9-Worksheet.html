3
My strategy — choose one and circle it
35–45 min
🛏 Bedroom rule
Phone charges outside the bedroom
The single highest-impact change in the research. Sleep and mood both improve.
✂ Follow audit
Unfollow three accounts
Unfollow the ones that consistently make you feel worse. You owe them nothing.
🔕 Notification off
Turn off one app's notifications
You open it on your terms. Not when it calls you. Small shift, big difference.
⏱ The pause
Ask before you open
"What am I looking for?" If you cannot answer, wait five minutes.
Q
Why did you choose that strategy in particular? What made the others less likely to work for you?
